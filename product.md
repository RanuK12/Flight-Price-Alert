# Flight Price Alert CLI

**Price: $19 (one-time purchase)**

## What is it?

A lightweight, pip-installable command-line tool that monitors flight prices and alerts you when they drop below your set threshold. Perfect for travelers who want to book at the best price without constantly checking websites.

## Features

- **Simple CLI** – No complex UI, just one command with your route and budget.
- **Smart thresholds** – Set your target price and get notified when flights hit it.
- **Date range support** – Check prices across a flexible window of days.
- **Lightweight & fast** – Runs locally; no cloud dependency or sign-up required.
- **Easy install** – `pip install flight-alert-cli` and you're set.
- **Logging included** – Alerts saved to a log file for reference.

## Who is it for?

- Budget travelers looking for deals
- Digital nomads planning trips
- Frequent flyers optimizing costs
- Anyone tired of checking flight prices manually

## Example use cases

- Monitor JFK → LAX for $250 or less between July 1–15, 2026
- Watch LHR → BCN for $300 off in early August
- Track domestic routes for seasonal price drops
